Riders

Behind every race result is the dedication, preparation, and determination of the riders who represent Delta T Racing on track.

What makes Delta T Racing unique is that our riders all share a professional background in the refrigeration industry. The same technical mindset, attention to detail, and problem-solving skills that they use in their daily work also carry over to the track — from fine-tuning bike setups to analysing performance and constantly striving for improvement.

Our team competes across different classes, with each rider bringing their own experience, riding style, and racing goals. From newcomers building their first seasons of experience to riders pushing for competitive results, every member of the team contributes to the growth and spirit of Delta T Racing.
